// 02 ABOUT

Me

I'm a final-year undergraduate at BITS Pilani, studying Electronics and Communications Engineering. I'm an aspiring researcher, currently exploring cooperative embodied agent systems, multi-drone routing and computer vision, and generally trying to expand what I know and can build.

How I tend to get into most topics is through tangents while reading papers or working on something else, something that sometimes works out for the worse than for the better, but here's to hoping that it all ties up neatly for me. I often find myself doing new courses or reading papers on topics that I find interesting, with the latest ones being related to attention in multi-agent systems and multi-agent reinforcement learning.

As a result of my degree I also have an interest in working on communication efficient systems, which was the inspiration for my latest publication involving bandwidth constrained cooperative VLN agents. I'll be adding my favourite papers/projects that I've found along with anything interesting I've found in them to my blog, so go check that out if you're interested. I'm also always looking for new sources of inspiration and avenues for collaboration, so if you have any ideas or want to work on something together, feel free to reach out!

Outside of the lab, I love watching movies, reading (if I can inspire myself to) and discovering new music. You should see a little icon somewhere on the page for what song I last liked, think of it as a recommendation to check out, and also a dedicated page for the media I've consumed recently, do check out any of them if they seem interesting, and be sure to drop recommendations! I also loving gaming, with my favourite games being the Mass Effect series, Minecraft and the Arkham Knight series. Might also add some photos I've taken cause who doesn't like a good view. I also love to work out, cause there's nothing like picking and dropping a heavy object to make you feel good :) If any of my interests also seem interesting to you, feel free to contact me to talk about them as well!
